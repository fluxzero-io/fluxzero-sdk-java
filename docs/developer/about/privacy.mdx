# Privacy Notice — Fluxzero

**Version:** May 2026

## 1. Who Are We and What Do We Offer?

We are Fluxzero B.V. ("Fluxzero", "we", "us"). We provide Fluxzero Cloud, our managed cloud runtime platform for backend applications and services. Customers can build application logic using the SDK, CLI and their own third-party AI coding tools, while Fluxzero provides runtime, execution, scaling, persistence, deployment, observability and platform-level security capabilities. The Platform, Fluxzero Cloud, Website, dashboard, support services if agreed, and related services expressly made available by Fluxzero are collectively referred to as our Services. Fluxzero Materials and related intellectual property rights are owned by Fluxzero IP B.V. or its licensors.

The SDK, CLI, Documentation, Generated Projects and other developer tooling may be made available to support use of the Platform and are subject to their applicable terms and licenses.

Free local development and testing functionality may be available. Fees apply when you activate a paid plan, create a Cloud Deployment or otherwise order paid Services. Self-Hosted Deployments, bring-your-own-cloud deployments and other custom deployment models are not part of the standard managed Services unless separately agreed in writing. Additional terms, responsibilities and technical requirements may apply.

Third-party AI coding tools are not part of the Services unless expressly provided by Fluxzero. If you use third-party AI coding tools, you do so under your own account or arrangement with the relevant provider.

## 2. Personal Data and Controller

We process certain Personal Data. Personal Data means all information by which a natural person can be directly or indirectly identified. Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), which applies to us, we are the Controller of your Personal Data for the processing activities described in this Privacy Notice.

If you process Personal Data through applications you build and run on the Platform, you remain the Controller for the processing of such Personal Data, and Fluxzero acts as a Processor on your behalf under the GDPR. The terms governing that relationship are set out in our Data Processing Agreement.

Customer Application Data means the contents and functional data of an application, such as records, files, messages, events, workflows, transactions, inputs, outputs, search data and index data. Platform Metadata means technical and operational information about the access to, use, performance, security and operation of the Platform and Services, such as timestamps, IP addresses, tenant identifiers, user identifiers, resource identifiers, logs, traces, usage metrics, error information, security events, deployment status and billing-related usage data.

Fluxzero does not use Customer Application Data or Personal Data contained in applications for product analytics, AI training, model training or general service improvement, unless Customer has expressly instructed or authorised Fluxzero to do so. Fluxzero may process limited Platform Metadata as an independent controller for account administration, billing, security, abuse prevention, compliance, diagnostics, service operation and improvement of the Platform. Where reasonably possible, Fluxzero uses aggregated or pseudonymised Platform Metadata for service improvement. Fluxzero does not use Platform Metadata to analyse Customer's non-public business logic, end-user behaviour or commercially sensitive use cases, except as necessary to provide, secure, support or improve the Services.

## 5. Cookies

We use functional and/or analytical cookies on our Website. A cookie is a simple small text file that can be stored in your device when visiting the Website. This text file identifies your browser and/or device. When visiting our Website again, the cookie ensures that your browser/device is recognized.

We use essential cookies to run our Website, including for authentication, security and load balancing. We may use analytics, such as Google Analytics, and session replay or heatmap tools, such as Microsoft Clarity, only where permitted by law and, where required, after your consent via our cookie banner or cookie settings.

We do not use advertising, retargeting or social media tracking cookies unless we update this Privacy Notice and, where required, request your prior consent.

We may use third-party providers for website functionality, fonts, status information, security, hosting, analytics-free operational measurements or similar operational purposes. These providers may receive limited technical information such as IP address, browser information and request metadata when your browser connects to their services.

You can adjust or withdraw your consent at any time via the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer of our Website, where applicable.

For more information on our use of cookies, please refer to our Cookie Notice: https://fluxzero.io/docs/about/cookies/.

## 6. How Long Do We Keep Personal Data?

We will not process or store any Personal Data that we do not need. We store Personal Data for as long as we need it for the above purposes, unless we are legally required to retain the Personal Data for a longer period. In this respect, we apply the following retention periods:

- **Personal Data of Users**: We retain the Personal Data for 30 days after the User's account has been deleted.
- **Personal Data of Representatives**: We retain the Personal Data for 5 years after our professional relationship with you has ended.
- **Newsletters**: We retain the Personal Data used for sending newsletters until you unsubscribe from our newsletter.
- **Personal Data in our correspondence**: We retain the Personal Data as long as necessary to handle your message/question/complaint and for a period of 2 years thereafter.
- **Personal Data in our records for the (tax) authorities**: We retain the Personal Data for 7 years, unless we are legally obliged to retain the data for a longer period.
- **Personal Data of Applicants**: We retain the Personal Data for a maximum of 4 weeks after the application process, unless the Applicant has given us permission to store the Personal Data for (up to) 1 year.
- **Service logs**: We retain service logs for 90 days after generation, unless a longer period is required for security investigations.

After expiry of the aforementioned retention period(s), we may process and retain certain Personal Data to comply with legal retention obligations and/or for fraud/misuse investigations or to substantiate a (judicial) claim. In such events, we will retain the Personal Data separately and only use such Personal Data for the aforementioned purposes.

## 13. Your Rights as a Data Subject and Our Contact Data

You have the right to:

- **Access**: You can ask us to provide access to, and a copy of, your personal data along with additional relevant details.
- **Data portability**: You can ask us to give you your personal data in a format that you can easily use elsewhere.
- **Rectification**: You can ask us to rectify any inaccuracies or complete any incompleteness in your personal data.
- **Restrict processing**: You can ask us to stop using your personal data, but still keep it stored.
- **Erasure**: You can ask us to erase your personal data, a right often referred to as "the right to be forgotten".
- **Withdraw consent**: You can ask us to stop using your personal data for the purpose we requested your consent for. This does not impact the legality of any prior or other uses of your data.
- **Object**: You can ask us to stop using your personal data for things like direct marketing or for our own business needs.

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at [privacy@fluxzero.io](mailto:privacy@fluxzero.io). We may ask to verify your identity. We will respond within one month of receiving your request.
